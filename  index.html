The Best Movies According To Francesco

My top three movies of all-time.

Split

A thriller about a man with multiple personality syndrome that has a darker secret waiting to be unfolded.

Interstellar

Set in a dystopian future,a group of astronauts travel through a wormhole in search of a new home for mankind.

Harry Potter & The Sorcerers Stone

A young wizard embarks on a new adventure when he gets accepted to a witchcraft and wizardry school not knowing the obstacles awaiting him.
